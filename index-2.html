MILE HIGH COATINGS

How We Started

After graduating in 2006 from Colorado State University in Fort Collins, David started Mile High Coatings in 2008. He began, quite literally, from the ground up, by knocking on doors, during what was the beginning of the Great Recession.


Through dedication and persistence, while many similar companies were failing and closing, David grew his customer base by providing excellent customer service, fair prices and quality paint jobs. Knowing that there was more to the industry, he started researching epoxy applications.


After attending and participating in training seminars, talking to product sales reps and learning from others with experience, Mile High Coatings started installing epoxy coatings on residential garage floors. At this point, one thing led to another and the reputation for high-quality, expert concrete coatings applications grew. The growth in this niche led to other opportunities, including staining concrete, commercial floors and starting in 2015, garage storage solutions. David still runs the day-to-day operations.
